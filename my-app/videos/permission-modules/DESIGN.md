## Style Prompt

A restrained technical explainer for developer tooling permissions. The video should feel like a clear product walkthrough: dark graphite canvas, frosted permission modules, precise motion, and calm warning accents. The viewer should understand the hierarchy from left to right without relying on subtitle captions.

## Colors

- Background: `#101416`
- Surface: `#172025`
- Surface elevated: `#202B31`
- Foreground: `#F2F5EF`
- Muted text: `#AEB8B4`
- Safe accent: `#78D08B`
- Review accent: `#E3B65F`
- Risk accent: `#ED786A`

## Typography

- Primary: PingFang SC or STHeiti for Chinese text.
- Secondary: Inter or system sans for small labels and numerals.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use subtitle-style full sentence captions at the bottom.
- Do not use neon purple-blue gradients or decorative orb backgrounds.
- Do not crowd the three modules; each permission level needs its own stable area.
- Do not make "full access" visually attractive; it should read as powerful but risky.
